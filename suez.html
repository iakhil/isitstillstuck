What is this about?

On March 23rd, a 220'000 tonne container ship got stuck on the Suez Canal. All shipping traffic is currently interrupted.

Ships that decide to go around will take an extra 9 days longer for US destinations and 14 days longer for European destinations.

Facts

  • Ship name: Ever given
  • Built: 2018
  • Size: 400m long / 59m wide
  • Gross tonnage: 219,079
  • Capacity: 20,388 TEUs (20ft container equivalents)
  • Owner : Shoei Kisen Kaisha (Japan)

How much is this costing?

Besides all the extra time you will have to wait for your new CPU/GPU/ Japanese stationary to arrive, this will cost shipping companies millions of dollars.

$9.6bn of daily ship traffic is currently interrupted
